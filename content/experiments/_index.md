---
title: "Experiments"
description: "Focused experiments testing specific hypotheses, architectures, or training techniques."
---

Small, scoped experiments designed to answer one question at a time.

The emphasis here is on learning velocity, not polished results. Expect:

- Ablation studies and hyperparameter sweeps
- Architecture comparisons on controlled benchmarks
- Debugging sessions and failure analysis
- Quick tests of ideas from papers before committing to a full project

Negative results are documented. If something didn't work, it's worth knowing why.
